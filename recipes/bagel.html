Fruit Chopped Bagel

This fruit chopped bagel delivers perfect bites all around. Filled with a quickly chopped-together cream cheese and fruit spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 plain bagel, sliced in half horizontally
  • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon confectioner's sugar
  • 1/2 cup mixed fruit, such as pineapple, strawberries, melon

Steps

  1. Toast bagel until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, slightly spread cream cheese on a cutting board. Add powdered sugar and fruit and chop and mix until chunky and well incorporated.
  3. Gather mixture together; scrape up with a spatula or bench scraper. Spread mixture onto bottom of toasted bagel and place top piece on top. Serve immediately.
